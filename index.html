Civil Engineering at its Best

RAMTAI NIGERIA COMPANY LIMITED is a Nigerian indigenous company registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria to undertake civil engineering construction jobs and allied services. It was incorporated on the 8th of July 1985 under the Companies Ordinance Act (Cap 32) as a Private Limited Liability Company with RC Number: 73,412. The main aim of establishing the firm is to produce a Nigerian company of international standard and world-class services. Thus, this vision has been our guiding principle for over two decades and we have been able to provide world- class standard services for our numerous clients over the years. RAMTAI NIGERIA COMPANY LIMITED has four major branches in Nigeria, namely; Abuja, Port-Harcourt, Kano and Ilorin with its’ corporate headquarters in Lagos. Our main business focus is on four major areas of construction and its’ allied services, viz-a-viz;

  • Civil Engineering Construction
  • Importation of Steel
  • Manufacturing of Building Materials
  • Real Estate and Property Development
  • Electrical and Mechanical Design
  • Road Construction and Rehabilitation
  • Geotechnical Services
With our pool of highly trained human resource consisting of 40 permanent staff members and hundreds of ad-hoc staff as occasion demands, our performance in all areas of our business interests has been excellent. Hence, our mission of attaining the status of an international one-stop shop in the construction industry is progressively being met.
